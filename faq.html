  • Our Commitment to Carbon Offsetting

    At HOMEiStudent, we're dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact. That's why we've proudly partnered with Stripe Climate, contributing 1% of our profits to cutting-edge carbon removal technologies and other vital climate change prevention strategies.

    Our ambitious goal is to become fully carbon negative by 2030, actively working to create a more sustainable future for generations to come.

  • What is Stripe Climate?

    Stripe Climate is a collective of businesses committed to accelerating the development and deployment of carbon removal technologies. These technologies are crucial for addressing climate change by permanently removing carbon dioxide from the atmosphere.

  • How does HOMEiStudent's contribution make a difference?

    Our 1% contribution to Stripe Climate directly supports a portfolio of highly innovative carbon removal projects. These initiatives are rigorously vetted by a team of scientific experts to ensure maximum impact and effectiveness in combating climate change.
