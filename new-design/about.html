Why HOMEi Exists

January 2023. James Dudson was living in chaos. Missing groceries. Messy rotas. Awkward money talks.

As a former HMO manager with an MSc in Real Estate, he knew this wasn't just his problem. Over half of students say housemate conflicts hurt their grades and wellbeing.

We tested HOMEi with 600+ students across 8 UK universities. They confirmed it: students need one app that handles everything, not five separate tools.

So we built it. Launched in Sheffield, September 2025. Got 1,200+ downloads in two weeks.
