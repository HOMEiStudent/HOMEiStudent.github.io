The Housemate Conflict Epidemic

Let's be honest: shared living is broken.

Over half of students say housemate conflicts hurt their grades and mental health. Arguments about bills, chores, and who ate whose food create a toxic environment exactly when you need peace and focus most.

The group chat becomes a battlefield. Friendships get strained. And somehow, taking out the bins becomes a political issue.

We knew there had to be a better way.

üí°

One App to End the Chaos

HOMEi was born from real frustration. Our founder James was living the chaos himself - missing groceries, messy rotas, awkward money conversations.

With experience as an HMO manager and an MSc in Real Estate, he understood the problem from every angle. So we tested with 600+ students across UK universities. They all said the same thing:

"We need ONE app that handles everything."

So we built it. Launched September 2025. 1,200+ downloads in two weeks proved we were onto something.
