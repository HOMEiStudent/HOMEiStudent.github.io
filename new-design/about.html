Why Student Houses Need Help

Managing a shared house is a lot of work.

In our survey of 600+ students, over half said house admin affects their studies and wellbeing. Bills, chores, and shopping all need organising, and it's easy to lose track.

That's time and mental energy you'd rather spend on your degree, your social life, and enjoying uni.

We knew there had to be a better way.

üí°

One App for Everything

HOMEi was born from real experience. Our founder James was juggling spreadsheets, group chats, and scribbled notes as a student just to keep track of house admin.

So we tested with 600+ students across UK universities. They all said the same thing:

"We need ONE app that handles everything."

So we built it. Launched September 2025. 1,200+ downloads in two weeks proved we were onto something.
