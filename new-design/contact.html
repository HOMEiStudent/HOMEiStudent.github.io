Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is HOMEi free to use?

Yes! HOMEi is completely free for students. Download it from the App Store or Google Play and start organizing your household today.

How many housemates can join?

You can add as many housemates as you need. Whether you're in a 2-person flat or a 10-person house, HOMEi scales with you.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal information with third parties.

Can I use HOMEi after graduation?

Of course! While we built HOMEi for students, anyone sharing a home can benefit from our features.
