Get Started in 2 Minutes

Setting up your house is quick and painless. Here's how it works.

Step 1: Download the App

HOMEi is available on both iOS and Android. Download it and get started straight away.

Just download and you're ready to go.

App Store Google Play

Step 2: Create Your House

Sign up with your email and create a new household. Give it a name (bonus points for creativity).

You'll get a unique house code that you can share with your housemates.

  • Quick signup that takes 30 seconds
  • Name your house whatever you want
  • Get a shareable invite code

Step 3: Invite Your Housemates

Share your house code with everyone who lives with you. They download the app, enter the code, and they're in.

Works with any number of housemates, from 2 to 20.

  • Share via WhatsApp, text, or just tell them
  • Housemates join instantly with the code
  • Everyone can see and contribute to the house

Step 4: Start Living Better

You're all set! Start adding chores, splitting bills, and creating shared lists.

With everything organised, you'll have more time to focus on your studies and actually enjoy student life.

  • Set up your first chore rota
  • Add any outstanding bills
  • Create a shopping list for the big shop
  • Enjoy the peace
