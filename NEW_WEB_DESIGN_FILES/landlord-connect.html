Landlord Connect

Better communication. Faster maintenance. Total privacy.

The renting experience, finally fixed

Reporting a broken boiler shouldn't mean digging through old emails. Asking about your deposit shouldn't mean a week of waiting. And your private life shouldn't be shared with your landlord.

That's why we're building Landlord Connect — a way to handle the landlord side of renting through HOMEi, while keeping everything else private.

What You'll Get

Everything that makes renting smoother, in one place.

📸

Photo Maintenance Reports

Snap a photo of the issue, add a quick description, and send. Your landlord gets everything they need in one tap — no more trying to describe a leak over text.

💬

Direct Messaging

Message your landlord through the app. Every conversation in one secure place — no more losing important messages in WhatsApp.

⏱️

Track Repair Status

See when your landlord has acknowledged the issue, when work is scheduled, and when it's complete. Full visibility, zero guessing.

📄

Tenancy Documents

Your contract, inventory, and important paperwork — all in one place. No more frantic searching when you need them.

🔔

Important Notifications

Get notified about scheduled maintenance, inspections, and key dates. Never miss something important from your landlord again.

⭐

Issue History

A full record of every issue you've reported and how it was resolved. Helpful evidence for when you move out and want your deposit back.

Privacy isn't a feature. It's the foundation.

We built HOMEi to be your private space. That doesn't change with Landlord Connect.

🔒

What stays 100% private

Your landlord cannot see any of your personal or household information. Even if they're on Landlord Connect, your private life stays private.

🚫

Your landlord NEVER sees

  • Your bills, expenses or splits
  • Your chore rotas or who does what
  • Your personal calendar (lectures, social events)
  • Your shared house calendar
  • Your shopping lists
  • Your housemate group chats
  • Your contributions or financial data
  • Your housemates' personal data
  • Anything you don't explicitly send to them
✅

Your landlord ONLY sees

  • Maintenance reports you choose to send
  • Messages you send to them directly
  • Property issues you flag
  • Documents they upload for you
✨

Your landlord doesn't use HOMEi? You don't lose anything.

HOMEi works perfectly as your free personal and shared house organiser whether your landlord joins or not. All current features stay free forever. Landlord Connect is just a bonus when your landlord opts in.

How It'll Work

Three simple steps to take the stress out of dealing with your landlord.

1

Your Landlord Joins

When your landlord signs up to HOMEi, you'll see them in your app. If they don't join, no worries — HOMEi still works exactly as it does today.

2

Connect Your Tenancy

Link your tenancy in the app with one tap. You stay in full control of what gets shared — nothing is automatic.

3

Report, Message, Resolve

Send maintenance reports, message your landlord, and track everything from one place. Your housemates only see house stuff. Your landlord only sees what you send.

Common Questions

Can my landlord see my bills, chores, or calendar?

No, never. HOMEi Landlord Connect is built privacy-first. Your landlord can ONLY see maintenance reports and messages you send to them directly. They have zero access to your bills, chore rotas, calendar, shopping lists, housemate chats, or any personal data.

Do I need a HOMEi-connected landlord to use the app?

Not at all. HOMEi works as a free personal and household organiser whether or not your landlord uses it. Landlord Connect is an optional bonus feature for when your landlord joins.

When will Landlord Connect launch?

We're actively building it. Join our waitlist below and we'll let you know the moment it's ready — you'll be the first to try it.

Will Landlord Connect cost extra?

No. HOMEi remains 100% free for students. All current features stay free, and Landlord Connect will be free for students too.

What if my landlord refuses to use it?

That's fine — HOMEi works perfectly without them. You'll still have all the bill splitting, chore rotas, calendars and household features. Landlord Connect just won't be active for your tenancy.

Can I leave a tenancy connection if I want to?

Yes. You can disconnect from your landlord at any time. Your data stays yours, and your housemates can use HOMEi independently of any landlord connection.
